Hi, my name is Alistair Folster. I developed this using Flask and Flask-SocketIO on the backend, and plain old JS/CSS on the frontend - no React, no frameworks.

The seat mechanic, which easily allows two people on the same computer or two people on different continents to play without needing separate game modes, was inspired by go.kahv.io.

The move sound effects were clipped from here. The game end sound effect is Chess.com's; all rights go to them.
